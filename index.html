What is worldbuilding?

Worldbuilding is the process by which fiction writers create their settings, whether it be Fantasy, Sci-fi, or an alternate version of our own world. It is one of my favorite aspects of fictional settings and few things make me lose interest in a fictional setting faster than poor worldbuilding. if you want to figure out how to build your own planet I would suggest this channel they are all about building worlds as well as Worldbuilding!
